About Us

A humble dream of a humble Management, with a commitment to excel in every aspect of strengthening the future generation and pursuit of highest standard in Physical, Psychological, Moral and Intellectual development of students, was culminated in the form of NAV BHARATH VIDYALAYA in a small hut in October 1988, with an aggregate strength of 15 students. From that humble beginning which became a giant leap in its own stride, there has been no looking back.

The school obtained recognition for Matriculation from the Govt. of Tamil Nadu in the Year 1992. And the school was upgraded to Higher Secondary in the year 1997.

We are proud to say that we have produced CENTUM RESULT in the Matriculation and Higher Secondary Examinations.
